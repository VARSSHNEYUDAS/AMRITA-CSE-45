About Us:

DeFortress is a leading global integrated security company, committed to delivering security products, services, and solutions to businesses across industries. We have the presence at 2000+ customer facilities in 19 States and union territories across India. Our strong ManTech Security solutions and remote monitoring facility from a global command centre help fortify our operations.

Being the top provider of security solutions, we maintain a comprehensive database of efficient professionals for catering to your manpower requirements. We combine state-of-the-art technology with a highly trained workforce to offer the most efficient security solutions that meet your specifications. We also have a specialized recruitment process and training module for the respective sectors and situations to which our security team gets deployed.

We value your demands and offer customized modern security services. To ensure this, we provide advanced training to our security guards on the latest safety measures and equipment so that they can prevent industrial hazards or accidents in your workplace.






















Protect Your Business with the Right Security Solutions

With over three decades of industry experience, you can trust us to deliver the right solution for your specific needs and proactively secure your places, people, and assets.
